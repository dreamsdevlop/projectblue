About Our Team

Meet our expert team of property advisors and legal consultants who have been serving Pakistan's real estate sector with integrity and excellence.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Shahid Mehmood Property Adviser & Consultant has emerged as Pakistan's most trusted name in property verification and legal consultation. Our journey began with a simple mission: to make property transactions transparent, secure, and legally compliant for every Pakistani.

Over the years, we have successfully verified over 15,000 properties and facilitated transactions worth billions of rupees. Our team of legal experts, property advisors, and documentation specialists work tirelessly to ensure that every property deal is conducted with the highest standards of integrity and professionalism.

Today, we are proud to be the preferred choice for individuals, investors, and businesses seeking reliable property consultation services across Pakistan. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction continues to drive our growth and success.
